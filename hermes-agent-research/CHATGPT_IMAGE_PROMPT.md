# ChatGPT Image-Prompt für LinkedIn-Post zu Hermes Agent

> Direkt in **ChatGPT (mit GPT Image / DALL·E 3)**, **Midjourney v7** oder **Sora Image** kopieren. Drei vollständige Konzepte plus ein technisches Spezifikations-Block. Englisch, weil Image-Modelle in Englisch deutlich präziser arbeiten.

---

## EMPFEHLUNG VORAB

**Format-Pick für LinkedIn:**
- **1200 × 1200 px (1:1 quadratisch)** für maximalen Feed-Reach (mobile dominiert) — primäre Wahl
- 1200 × 627 px (1.91:1) wenn der Post als externer Link mit Vorschau geteilt wird

**Stil-Pick:** Konzept 1 (mythologisch-modern) hat den höchsten Scroll-Stopper-Effekt und ist visuell nicht austauschbar mit anderen KI-Posts. Wenn dein Feed nüchterner ist → Konzept 3 (Data-Architecture).

## KONZEPT 1 — „Mythos trifft Server-Rack" (EMPFEHLUNG)

**Idee:** Hermes (griechischer Götterbote) steht zwischen Marmor-Antike und glühender Server-Infrastruktur. Verbindet das Branding („Hermes") mit dem Tech-Kern. Visuell sofort wiedererkennbar, hebt sich von Standard-„AI-Robot-Hand"-Bildern ab.

```
A cinematic editorial illustration in the style of a high-end tech magazine
cover (think MIT Technology Review meets Wired). Centered composition: the
Greek god Hermes — a young, athletic male figure with winged sandals and a
glowing caduceus staff — stands in three-quarter view. His left side dissolves
into classical white marble (Doric column fragments, soft museum lighting,
subtle dust particles in light beams). His right side dissolves into a modern
data center: warm amber server rack lights, fiber-optic cables glowing teal,
soft volumetric haze, holographic UI panels floating beside him showing
abstract memory layers and skill icons.

The caduceus staff is reimagined: the two intertwined snakes are replaced by
flowing strands of data — translucent ribbons of code and tiny glowing nodes,
ascending toward small wing motifs at the top.

Color palette: deep midnight navy background (#0B1220), warm amber accents
(#F5A623), cool teal data glow (#00D4C8), aged marble white (#EFE9DD), with
subtle Greek-key meander patterns barely visible in the background.

Lighting: dramatic side-light from the right (data-center side), soft fill
from the left (marble side). Volumetric god-rays, lens flare on the staff.
Shallow depth of field, bokeh in background.

Mood: confident, mythic, intelligent — not aggressive or sci-fi. Editorial
elegance, not stock-photo corporate.

No text in the image. No logos. No on-screen UI text. No legible code.
No watermarks.

Composition: rule of thirds, figure on the right vertical line, leaving
negative space on the left for LinkedIn post overlay if needed. 
Subject occupies roughly 60% of frame height.

Aspect ratio: 1:1 square, 1200x1200 px, ultra-detailed,
photorealistic-illustration hybrid, 8K quality, sharp focus on face and staff.

Reference style cues: Greg Rutkowski lighting, James Jean composition,
Beeple atmospheric depth — but cleaner and more editorial.
```

## KONZEPT 2 — „Der wachsende Agent" (Metapher-Stark)

**Idee:** Visualisiert die These „grows with you". Ein dunkler Bildschirm, aus dem ein leuchtender Baum aus Code/Datenströmen wächst. Sehr metaphorisch, gut für eine These wie *„Wir hören auf, KI-Tools zu kaufen — wir trainieren KI-Mitarbeiter."*

```
A minimalist conceptual illustration. A jet-black laptop sits open on a dark
desk, screen glowing softly. From the screen, growing upward and outward, is
a luminous tree made entirely of flowing code, data nodes, and translucent
neural-network pathways. Each branch ends in small glowing fruits — tiny
geometric icons representing skills (gear, key, scroll, message bubble,
calendar, magnifying glass) softly pulsing.

The tree's roots extend downward INTO the laptop screen, suggesting the
agent grows from within the machine, not on top of it. Some roots also
extend out across the desk and into the floor, hinting at distributed
infrastructure.

A small, elegant winged-sandal motif (Hermes symbol) glows faintly on the
laptop lid as a subtle brand cue.

Color palette: deep black/charcoal base (#0A0A0F), warm gold-amber for the
tree's main glow (#FFB347), cool cyan highlights on data nodes (#5EEAD4),
soft purple atmospheric haze in the far background (#3B1F5E).

Lighting: the tree is the only light source. Strong rim-light on the laptop
edges. Soft volumetric glow filling the upper third of the frame. Long, soft
shadows.

Mood: quiet, intelligent, organic-meets-digital. Suggests slow but
compounding growth — patience and depth, not hype.

No text, no logos, no UI elements with readable copy. Negative space in the
upper-left third for typography overlay.

Composition: laptop in lower third, tree filling upper two-thirds, slightly
off-center to the right (for compositional balance). 1:1 square aspect ratio,
1200x1200 px. Ultra-detailed, sharp focus on the tree, slight motion-blur
in the rising data particles. Editorial illustration style, clean lines,
not photorealistic.

Reference style cues: Apple keynote visuals, Stripe illustrations, but
darker and more atmospheric.
```

## KONZEPT 3 — „Three-Layer Memory" (für die Engineer-Bubble)

**Idee:** Saubere, technische Visualisierung der 3-Schichten-Memory-Architektur. Spricht direkt CTOs, Engineers und ML-Leute an. Wirkt informierter als die typischen „glowing brain"-Bilder.

```
A high-end isometric technical illustration of a three-layer translucent
data architecture, floating in dark cosmic space. Three horizontal glowing
slabs stacked vertically with slight rotation, each layer showing different
density and pattern:

TOP LAYER — labeled visually (no text) by floating chat-bubble icons and
flowing horizontal light streams: thin, fast, ephemeral. Color: cool white
with cyan edges. Represents Session Memory.

MIDDLE LAYER — denser grid of small glowing cubes connected by light-lines,
some cubes brighter than others. Slow particle drift. Color: warm amber
with gold edges. Represents Persistent Memory.

BOTTOM LAYER — fewer but much larger crystalline structures (like polished
geometric gems) anchored on a darker, more solid base, connected by thicker
golden threads to the layers above. Color: deep gold with white core glow.
Represents Skill Memory.

Thin vertical light beams connect all three layers, suggesting bidirectional
data flow. Soft particle systems flow between layers. The whole structure
floats above a faint grid plane that fades into infinite black space.

Color palette: deep space black background with subtle nebula purples
(#1A0F2E), warm amber-gold (#F5A623), cool electric cyan (#00E5FF),
crystalline white core (#FFFFFF).

Lighting: each layer self-illuminating. Strong volumetric god-rays where
beams connect layers. Subtle reflections on each slab's surface.

Mood: technical, sophisticated, premium SaaS aesthetic. Should feel like a
diagram from a Stripe/Linear/Vercel marketing page — but more atmospheric.

No text labels. No code. No logos. No UI chrome.

Composition: structure centered, occupying middle 70% of frame, isometric
30-degree angle, slight upward camera tilt for hero-shot feel. 1:1 square,
1200x1200 px. Ultra-detailed, photorealistic 3D render quality, ray-traced
lighting, sharp focus throughout.

Reference style cues: Octane Render aesthetic, Apple silicon launch visuals,
Linear app marketing illustrations.
```

## TECHNISCHE SPEZIFIKATION (an jedes Konzept anhängbar)

```
Render specifications:
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 (or 1.91:1 for link-preview format)
- Resolution: 1200x1200 px minimum, 4K preferred
- Style: editorial illustration / cinematic concept art / premium 3D render
- No text, no logos, no watermarks, no readable code
- LinkedIn-feed safe: appropriate for professional context, no aggressive
  imagery, no human faces in close-up unless illustration style
- Color contrast must remain readable when LinkedIn applies its automatic
  feed-darkening filter
- Avoid: AI-cliché elements (glowing blue brain, robot hand, generic neural
  net mesh, "binary code rain"), overused stock-photo aesthetics, garish
  rainbow gradients, Comic Sans-equivalent type
```

## SO NUTZT DU DIE PROMPTS OPTIMAL

1. **In ChatGPT eingeben:** Wähle ein Konzept, kopiere den Block in eckigen Anführungszeichen, hänge die Technische Spezifikation an. Falls ChatGPT/DALL·E den Prompt zu sehr „glättet", füge davor: *„Use this prompt verbatim, do not paraphrase or shorten."*
2. **Iteration:** Lass ChatGPT 4 Varianten erzeugen. Wähle die beste, dann sag *„Same composition, but [adjustment]"* — z. B. mehr negative space, anderer Akzentton.
3. **Midjourney-Variante:** Hänge `--ar 1:1 --style raw --v 7 --stylize 250` an für Konzept 1, `--stylize 100` für Konzept 2 (sauberer), `--stylize 400` für Konzept 3 (maximale Render-Qualität).
4. **Upscale & Polish:** Nach der Generierung in **Topaz Photo AI** oder **Magnific** auf 4K hochskalieren — LinkedIn komprimiert Bilder hart, je höher die Ausgangsauflösung, desto besser sieht es im Feed aus.
5. **Negative Space prüfen:** Wenn du Text overlay machen willst (siehe Konzept 1 + 2), prüfe vor dem Posten in **Figma** oder **Canva**, ob der Overlay-Bereich wirklich frei genug für lesbaren Text ist.

## A/B-TEST-VORSCHLAG

Wenn du wirklich Reach optimieren willst: poste den Hauptpost mit **Konzept 1** (mythologisch — Scroll-Stopper). Repost 5–7 Tage später mit **Konzept 3** (technisch — andere Bubble) und einem leicht angepassten Hook. So bedienst du beide Zielgruppen, ohne in einem Post zu spagatieren.
